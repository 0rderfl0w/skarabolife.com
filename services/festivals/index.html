Adaptable kitchen support for festival teams

Festivals

Supporting your festival food operations with experience, enthusiasm, and adaptability, from prep to service, working seamlessly with your team.

*

The Idea

Festival kitchens need calm hands, clear communication, and food that can hold up under real event conditions. Lotte brings experience and adaptability to support prep, service, and plant-based menus in busy festival environments.

The work can flex around what your team already has in place: hands-on preparation, collaborative service, menu support, or practical kitchen help that keeps the food experience warm and reliable.

What This Can Include

  • Prep and service support for festival food teams
  • Adaptable help across changing event conditions
  • Plant-based menu experience for larger groups
  • Collaborative kitchen presence from setup to service

Tell Me About Your Plans

Share the dates, location, number of people, and the feeling you want the food to create. Lotte will help shape the next step from there.
